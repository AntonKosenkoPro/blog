# Coffee
## DF64 Gen 2.5 Coffee Grinder

**Price:** 300-350€

**Motivation:** Necessary device for a lot of coffee styles (turkish, cold brew, espresso etc.) and preserves coffee ungrinded (=fresh). 

**Link:** [Turin](https://www.turingrinders.com/products/turin-df64-gen-2-5-220v-single-dose-coffee-grinder-international-220v)

**Alternatives:** 
- [DF54](https://www.turingrinders.com/products/turin-df54-220v-single-dose-flat-burr-grinder-international-220v-with-eu-plug) - it's cheaper (200-250€) but DF64 have better performance and reliability. In wast of cases they are the same.
- [Timemore C5 Pro ESP](https://sigmacoffee-eu.com/collections/timemore/products/timemore-chestnut-c5-esp-pro-manual-coffee-grinder) - manual one (95€). Not quite suitable for espresso or turkish coffee because grinding will be very time consuming.
